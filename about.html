About Finbee

Finbee Financial Services helps SMEs, entrepreneurs and professionals build stronger, smarter and more financially resilient businesses.

Founded and led by CA Sandeep Pugalia, Finbee combines over 20 years of banking, credit, real estate finance and SME advisory experience with modern, AI-enabled consulting frameworks.


Our Beliefs

  • Clarity comes first: No strategy works if the numbers are unclear.
  • Capital should support growth, not stress: Funding must match cash flow.
  • Systems scale businesses: Processes, controls and dashboards enable growth.
  • Advisory must be practical: Every recommendation should work in real life.

Founder’s Note

“After working across banks, investment banking and SME advisory for more than two decades, I saw the same pattern repeat: good businesses struggling because their finances were not structured, monitored or aligned with their goals. I built Finbee to change that — to help Indian SMEs grow with clarity and confidence.”

– CA Sandeep Pugalia

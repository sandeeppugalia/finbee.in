Our Services

We focus on four key pillars that cover the financial life of a growing business.

Business Finance Advisory

We help you structure and secure the right kind of funding:

  • Working capital limits & term loans
  • Project finance & expansion funding
  • Real estate & construction finance
  • Cash-credit, overdraft and structured facilities
  • Bank-ready projections, CMA data and documentation

Outcome: Better approval chances, better structure, lower stress.

Capital Strategy & Planning

Design a capital structure that supports growth instead of blocking it:

  • Capital requirement assessment
  • Debt vs internal accrual planning
  • EMI and repayment planning
  • Interest cost optimisation
  • Cash flow forecasting and scenario planning

Outcome: A clear view of how your growth will be funded and repaid.

SME Growth Consulting

We work with you on the business side, not just the banking side:

  • Profitability analysis and leak diagnosis
  • Pricing, costing and margin improvement
  • Business model and product mix review
  • Process design, SOPs and internal controls
  • Dashboards, KPIs and review mechanisms

Outcome: A business that can scale beyond daily firefighting.

Compliance & Documentation Support

We assist with the financial and compliance backbone of your business:

  • Project reports, DPRs and viability studies
  • CMA data and banker documentation packs
  • Financial health reviews and ratio analysis
  • Guidance on subsidy- and scheme-linked documentation

Outcome: Clean, organised paperwork that supports faster decisions.
